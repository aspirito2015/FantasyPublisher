Standings

A publisher's PS (Publisher Score) is the sum of the GSVs (Game Score Values) of all games on that publisher's roster. The following formulas are used to calculate GSV, where OC = OpenCritic's Top Critic Average and RC+ = Review Count Avg Review Count :

GSV = OC * ( 1 + RC+ 100 ) + RC+
